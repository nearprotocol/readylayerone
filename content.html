2020 Open Blockchain Week

Content Guidelines

May 4-6th, 2020

Here are a few guidelines to consider as you submit content for a slot at Ready Layer One

Share New Content

Your talk should address new research, findings. Be resourceful.

Make it Actionable

Include either code or specific examples and case studies.

Make it Interactive

There are several options such as chat engagement, polls, and more!

Make it Relevant

Are there major issues we're seeing in today’s world that this technology or idea can help solve? If so, talk about it!

Make it Public

Share your topic in public forums (Twitter, Reddit, your medium of choice!). Call for feedback/participation from community members in advance of the event. If you tag @readylayerone we will retweet and amplify your reach.

Be Respectful

Please be ethical/sensible in your content. Be respectful of others. And remember - there’s no shilling allowed, so keep the focus on the tech and applications!
